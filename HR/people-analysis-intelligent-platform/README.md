# PeopleLens HR Intelligence Platform

A tech-focused enterprise HR intelligence platform inspired by Workday-style people analytics.

## Live dashboard

[View PeopleLens HR Intelligence Platform](https://phoebefu6.github.io/design-dashboard-with-phoebe/HR/people-analysis-intelligent-platform/)

## Features

- Workforce planning for tech job families
- Acquisition funnel management
- Resume summarization mock feature
- Resume-to-JD match scoring
- Interview case study generation
- Interview feedback aggregation and decision report
- Retention and attrition monitoring
- Learning and development coverage
- Onboarding and offboarding journey controls
- Tenure analytics
- Rewards and inclusion views
- Filters by company, 10 countries, and 10 tech departments

All data shown is illustrative for client-review and portfolio demonstration.
